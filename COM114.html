17. Permutation and Combination

Permutation: Arrangement of objects.
nPr = n! / (n-r)!

Combination: Selection of objects.
nCr = n! / \[r!(n-r)!\]

18. Worked Example on Permutation

Find the number of ways to arrange 3 students from 5 students.

5P3 = 5! / (5−3)! = 5 × 4 × 3 = 60

19. Worked Example on Combination

Select 2 students from 5 students.

5C2 = 5! / \[2!(5−2)!\] = 10

22. Worked Example on Probability

A die is thrown once. Find the probability of getting an even number.

Even numbers = {2,4,6}

P(Even) = 3/6 = 1/2
